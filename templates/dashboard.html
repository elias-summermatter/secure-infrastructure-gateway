Before you download

This file unlocks access to protected infrastructure. It contains a private cryptographic key that identifies you personally.
  • Anyone with this file can impersonate you on the gateway.
  • Treat it like a password: never share it, email it, paste it into chat, or commit it to git.
  • Store it only on devices you control — not shared drives or cloud sync folders.
  • If the file ever leaks, come straight back here and click Rotate — the old one is invalidated instantly.

Join the shared network?

While active, every other member can reach your machine directly over the WireGuard tunnel using your WG IP. They will see your username and WG IP in their peer list, same as you will see theirs.
  • The channel itself is secure — all traffic stays inside the WireGuard tunnel (end-to-end encrypted, post-quantum-hedged PSK). Nothing leaves the gateway, and clients outside the network cannot see it.
  • But your machine is exposed to the other members. If you're running an SSH / SMB / HTTP service that only listens on your WG interface, every active peer can try it. Host-firewall accordingly, or bind services to loopback only.
  • Activations last 1 h and can be extended up to 8 h, like any other service. When your grant expires you drop out of the network and in-flight connections to peers are cut.
  • Your admin can see and revoke your participation at any time.
